WHO WE ARE

Prime League is the latest training centre to assist ordinary citizens and sportsmen to be better performers, experience better health and live more powerfully. We employ ideal evidence-based programs, certified and trained instructors and a community with good health.

  • State-of-the-art equipment
  • Personalised coaching
  • Group classes & virtual training
  • Rehab & recovery support

WHAT WE DO

HIIT, strength, mobility, rehab, online classes, etc. — we ballpark programs to give results. Regardless of whether your goal is to lose weight, gain strength, recover after injury or train to be sport specific, our coaches will develop a program specifically to your needs.

  1. Assessment & goal-setting
  2. Personalised training plan
  3. Tracking & progress updates
  4. Community events & challenges
